About Guido van Rossum

10 Facts About Guido van Rossum

  • Guido van Rossum was born on January 31, 1956, in the Netherlands.
  • He is the creator of the Python programming language.
  • Guido began developing Python in 1989 during his Christmas holidays.
  • He named Python after the comedy group "Monty Python's Flying Circus."
  • He released Python 1.0 in 1991.
  • Guido van Rossum served as Python's Benevolent Dictator For Life (BDFL) until 2018.
  • He has worked for notable companies like Dropbox, Google, and Microsoft.
  • Guido retired in November 2020 but continues to be active in the programming community.
  • He holds a Master's degree in Mathematics and Computer Science from the University of Amsterdam.
  • Guido is widely respected as a pioneer in the open-source community.
